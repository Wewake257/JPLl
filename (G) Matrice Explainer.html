📖 Glossary of All Metrics
RTG21‑22 … RTG25‑26 Raw letter ratings from performance reviews.
Raw performance ratings (A*, A+, A, etc.) for 5 years.
avg_rating Average of numeric ratings.
Simple average of the 5 numeric ratings.
rating_trend Latest – First year.
Change in performance from first to last year.
rating_consistency Standard deviation of ratings.
Stability of performance; lower is better.
recency_weighted_rtg Weighted average giving more importance to recent years.
Weighted sum of ratings (latest year gets highest weight).
score_* Min‑max normalized to 0‑100 across all employees.
Normalised versions of key metrics, enabling fair combination.
composite_score Weighted sum of all normalized scores.
Single number summarising overall performance.
performance_tier Categorical label based on composite score thresholds.
Star Performer, Exceeds, Meets, Developing, Below Expectation.
